About Our Institution

We at the University of Ado nurture the talents of many creatives within the world to allow them the opportunity to become the best in their chosen field. Inspired by Ado, the utaite and J-Pop idol, we allow students to grow, live, and set a legacy as great as Ado.

Our facilities and teaching method allows students to be as creative and free as possible, while also instilling in them the knowledge they need to succeed. Ado's main wish as an idol is to play a supporting role in the lives of others and uplifting people using her song and music, which we have taken as our philosophy reagrding students.
